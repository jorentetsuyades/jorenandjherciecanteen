Menu We Offer

These are example dishes, and actual offerings may differ based on the meal time and product availability.

Breakfast

Usually served with rice, often garlic fried rice, and a fried egg – "silog" style

• Longsilog
• Siomaisilog
• Hamsilog
• Hotsilog
• Malingsilog

Also we are serving dishes like vegetable and chicken, beef, and fish:
• Tortang Talong (vegetable)
• Chicken Adobo
• Sarciadong Isda (Salmon)

Lunch

Heavier meals, often paired with rice and vegetables

• Pork Adobo
• Sinigang na Baboy
• Chicken Afritada
• Beef Nilaga
• Fried Tilapia

Also we are serving dishes like vegetable and chicken, beef, and fish:
• Pinakbet (vegetable)
• Tinolang Manok (chicken)
• Bistek Tagalog (beef)
• Miso(bangus) (fish)

Dinner

Often similar to lunch but with lighter or reheated dishes

• Daing na Isda
• Adobong Manok
• Giniling
• Inihaw na Liempo /Manok
• Tuyo and Rice

Also we are serving dishes like vegetable and chicken and fish:
• Ginisang Ampalaya (vegetable)
• Adobo (chicken or pork)
• Chicken Curry
• Piniritong Isda (Salmon)

About Us

At Joren and Jhercie's Canteen, we’re dedicated to fueling hardworking construction teams with hearty, affordable, and freshly prepared meals every day.
We understand the demands of the job site, so we serve nutritious dishes that keep you energized and ready to tackle any challenge.
Our commitment is to provide delicious, timely food in a clean and welcoming environment — because great work deserves great food.
